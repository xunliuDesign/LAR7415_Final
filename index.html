3rd VISION

What makes the urban space enjoyable? Emerging technologies enable us to vision our everyday urban space in more interactive, informative and objective ways. "3rd Vision" is an interactive installation project in 2019 Shenzhen Bi-City Biennale of Ubbanism\Architecture. This project tries to quantify the intangible qualities of the cities, based on the ratings from the visitors by using computer vision and machine learning. Learn about our methods.
